// Who are we?

We are a small group making the
web, just a bit better one project at a time

We spend our time to make products, mainly websites to enhance the average user's experience on the web better. Our products are free and open source most of the time (with some execptions) and are available at github.com/znci.
Everyone is encouraged to contribute, as that improves your experience as well as others.
Most of our prodcuts are under the MIT license.

// What do we do?

We make websites to make people's
experience on the web better.
  • [Azalea] A CSS framework to make a web developer's life easier, with inbuilt utility classes and pre-built components
  • [QR Generator] Easily make QR codes that link to websites through a convenient website.
  • [Paint] A quick and easy to use painting app, on the web!
  • [BetterPS] A simple and easy to use chrome extension which maes the PlayerServers website better.
  • [MusicBridge] A simple MacOS app to control spotify/apple music simply using MIDI
  • [Terminal] A vanilla JS library to make terminals on the web.
